About This Project

Technology helps people stay connected, informed, and independent in today’s digital world. As new devices and platforms continue to evolve, it can be difficult to keep up with the skills needed to use technology safely and confidently.

This project was created by students in the Media & Technology Club to provide simple, easy-to-follow technology guides designed for learners of all ages.

Our goal is to build confidence with technology and strengthen connections across generations.

How to Use This Website

Guides are organized by age group and everyday technology needs. Each topic includes a short video tutorial and a written guide. You may explore topics in any order depending on your needs.
