About This Project

Technology helps people stay connected, informed, and independent in today's digital world. As new devices and platforms continue to evolve, it can be difficult to keep up with the skills needed to use them safely and confidently.

This project was created by students in the Media & Technology Club to provide simple, easy-to-follow guides for learners of all ages — from young kids exploring the internet for the first time to seniors making their first video call.

How It Works

01 — Find Your Group

Jump to the section that's right for you — Kids, Teens & Adults, or Seniors.

02 — Watch the Video

Each topic has a short, easy-to-follow video. No prior knowledge needed.

03 — Read the Guide

Prefer reading? Every topic also has a written step-by-step guide.
